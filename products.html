Foundry Services

Foundry Services involve semiconductor manufacturers providing their manufacturing processes and facilities to other companies or designers. Designers bring in their designs, utilizing the manufacturing process to produce semiconductor chips. This enables emerging companies or design houses to bring products to market without the need to own advanced manufacturing facilities.

Semiconductor Design Services

Semiconductor manufacturers offer custom semiconductor design services tailored to the specific needs of their customers. This includes the design of application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) and system-on-chip (SoC) solutions. Customers can leverage design services provided by semiconductor manufacturers' experts to develop unique products and features.

Technical Support and Consulting

Semiconductor manufacturers provide technical support and consulting services to their customers. This includes assistance with device design, optimization of manufacturing processes, troubleshooting, and support for bringing products to market. Customers can leverage the specialized knowledge of semiconductor manufacturers to smoothly progress through the development and manufacturing processes of their products.
