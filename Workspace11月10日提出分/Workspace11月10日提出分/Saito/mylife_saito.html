A Day in Technical College

Morning

I wake up at 8 AM. Then, I arrive at school, where I attend classes for specialized subjects, general education courses, and work on my graduation project. The classes cover subjects such as mathematics, science, engineering, and computer science.

Lunch

During lunchtime, I have my meals at the dormitory's cafeteria.

Afternoon

In the afternoon, I continue with specialized subject classes, general education courses, and my graduation project, similar to the morning session.

Extracurricular Activities

After school, I either participate in the soccer team or work part-time.

Returning Home

At the end of the day, after taking a shower and roll call, I go to bed.

In Conclusion

A day in a technical college is filled with enriching learning experiences and opportunities for interaction. Balancing academics and extracurricular activities, I acquire new skills and knowledge, allowing me to grow. In one word, my life in technical college has been fantastic.
