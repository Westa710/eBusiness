My life in NIT,Tsuyama

What is NIT,Tsuyama?

NIT,Tsuyama is located in Tsuyama City, Okayama prefecuture.
you can learn specialized skills related to engineering and science here.

What I learn?

I'm in the Department of Integrated Science and Technology Information Engineering Program.
This course offers a variety of classes on information technology
For example...

  • Information system development
  • Algorithms and Data structure
  • Control engineering

In the fifth grade, we learn about advanced specialized technologies.
I am particularly interested in a lecture on UML, which is a diagram used
in system development for design, and there are many different types of diagrams.
By learning this, we will learn about practical system development methods.

Graduation research

As my graduation research, I am developing a microcomputer-based paddy weeding robot in the 曽利そり laboratory.
Currently, I am developing software for the weeding robot for field experiments.
For example, reading measurements from touch sensors and gyro sensors, creating user interfaces.

Club activity

I am a member of the track and field team, specializing in short distance.
I have been practicing track and field for 8 years continuously since I was in junior high school.
At the NIT,Tsuyama, I participated in various competitions such as the prefectural competitions
and the National NIT competitions.
Currently, I have changed my field to long distance and am training to finish the Okayama Marathon in November 2023!
